Bulgarian nature - Rhodopes



WARNING! We at DiscoverBulgaria recommend that you purchase travel / mountaineering insurance before embarking on mountain tourism. You continue at your own risk if you do not purchase one.



The Rhodopes occupy the eastern part of the Rila-Rhodope massif, in the central part of the Balkan Peninsula. The mountain stretches over the territory of Southern Bulgaria and Northern Greece.



Snezhanka Peak

The safest ways to reach the top are through the four lifts - Studenets - Snow White, Smolyan Lakes - Snow White, Raspberry - Snow White and Stoykite - Snow White. From the tower we can enjoy beautiful views.

Natural landmark Wonderful bridges

The incredible rock phenomenon near the village of Zabardo has a magnetic force. Huge, a semicircular gap forms the arch of the rock phenomenon, and beyond it are the majestic spruce forests, giving a unique contrast to the view.
