Bulgarian cities - Tryavna


TRYAVNA WEATHER

Tryavna is a town in Gabrovo District, Central Northern Bulgaria. It is the administrative and economic center of the municipality of the same name Tryavna. The population of the city according to the census in 2011 is 9426 inhabitants, which makes it the third largest settlement in the district. The preserved Revival architecture and the cultural heritage of this period attract many tourists.


In Tryavna can be found many sights such as


Clock Tower

The clock tower is the symbol of the town of Tryavna and was built in 1814. It is part of the only preserved Revival architectural ensemble in the country - Captain Grandfather Nicholas Square.

Museum of Carving and Ethnographic Art

From time immemorial Tryavna is famous for its skillful masters of carpentry, woodcarving and icon painting. Here is the only Museum of Carving Art in the country, and the old one part of the city has been turned into an architectural reserve.

Kivgiren Bridge

The power is the other emblematic symbol of Tryavna. It was built in 1844-1845. Initially it was made of wood, but after torrential rains the river came and often knocked it down. The construction was entrusted to Master Dimitar Cergyuv, who had been choosing the stone for a long time - to endure cold, heat and water element.

Old School Museum

Built in the period 1836-1839. One of the first secular schools in Bulgaria. It has been discovered as a mutual school in the autumn of 1839. In 1849 the people of Tryavna invited Petko as a teacher in Tryavna Slaveykov, who also opened a class school in this building. Introduces the study of music, drawing, classes in physical education.

Raikova House Museum

Built in 1846. It is a typical example of the Tryavna architectural school. Through In 1846 the Tryavna merchant Nikola Raykov entrusted the construction of his new house to the best Tryavna master at that time - Dimitar Sergyov. The exposition presents the Tryavna life from the end of the XIX and the beginning of the twentieth century. Here you will see the first bed brought to Tryavna, urban type house furniture.

Street of the Old Masters

The street of the old masters in Tryavna is PR Slaveykov Street. see various workshops, cafes that offer coffee on the sand, candy shops, shops with Tryavna underwear. On it is the house of the Slaveykov family, the Love Fountain, the Clock Tower, The Kivgiren Bridge, Daskalov House Museum.
