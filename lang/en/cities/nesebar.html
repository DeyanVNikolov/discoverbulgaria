Bulgarian cities - Nessebar


NESSEBAR WEATHER

Nessebar is a town in southeastern Bulgaria, located on the Black Sea coast. It is located in the municipality of Nessebar, Bourgas district, and in the vicinity to the north it almost merges with the largest Bulgarian Black Sea resort Sunny Beach. The city is the administrative center of the municipality of Nessebar and is one of the oldest cities in Europe, founded more than 3200 years ago.


In Nessebar you can find many sights such as


Windmill

The windmill is emblematic of the town of Nessebar and is a kind of symbol of the town. It is located on the bridge between the old and the new town in Nessebar. Around the rest of the Turkish time authentic a windmill has benches built, and the sea view makes it even more beautiful.

The statue of the Fisherman

At the entrance to the old town of Nessebar we are greeted by the famous and beloved windmill, the most famous emblem of the city. On the south side of the isthmus, however, rises the new symbol of Nessebar - the statue of the Fisherman holding a cross. It was erected in 2006 and is the work of sculptor Stavri Kalinov.

Ancient amphitheater

The ancient amphitheater in Nessebar is located only 2 km from the city center, in close to the port. On his stage they often perform as some of the best Bulgarian pop and pop singers, as well as foreign ones.

Archaeological Museum

The Museum in Nesebar is one of the most visited landmarks. It is stored here millennial history and is presented in a modern and attractive for visitors way.

Ethnographic Museum

The museum is housed in the house of Moskoyani, which is a typical architecture of the Black Sea coast from the late period of the Renaissance. The purpose of the exhibition at the Ethnographic Museum is to show, albeit in part the great art of the Bulgarian woman.

Aviation monument

The monument of aviation in Nessebar is an aircraft of the MiG-21 model, which is defended the country's security in the past. It was opened on the occasion of the 100th anniversary of the Air Force forces of Bulgaria.
