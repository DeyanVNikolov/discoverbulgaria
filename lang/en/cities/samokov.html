Bulgarian cities - Samokov


SAMOKOV WEATHER

Samokov is a town in southwestern Bulgaria. It is located in the Sofia region, 55 km from the city of Sofia and 10 km from the resort and tourist complex Borovets.


In Samokov you can find many sights such as


Museum of History

The Historical Museum in Samokov was established in 1930 as a collection of the Chitalishte-monument "Father Paisii". The real building of the museum was built in 1940 according to the project of the famous architect Yurdan Yurdanov. The museum has more than 30,000 exhibits, divided into collections: ethnographic, woodcarving, archeological, manuscripts and paintings.

Saraf's house

The history and the name of the Saraf House are connected with the Jewish family Arie, whose ancestor, born in Vienna, settled in 1793 in Samokov. The wealth and splendor of this enterprising family was reflected in their houses and in the construction of the Synagogue at a time when there are no significant public buildings in our lands and the external splendor of the home is still something new.

Chitalishte-monument "Father Paisii"

Chitalishte-monument "Father Paisii" is the oldest cultural institution in Samokov. The earliest information about the "reading room" in the library of the school is from the XVII century, and the seal of the community center is from 1859. In 1870 it received the name "Holy Septuagint", but did not have its own building and operated in the Metropolitanate.

Art Gallery

The Art Gallery presents the museum's collections of works of art, as well as jubilee exhibitions of famous Samokov artists - Slavi Genev, Naum Hadjimladenov, Vasil Zahariev, Hristo Yonchev-Kriskarets, Alexander Milenkov and others.

Umbrella fountain

It is named after its unusual shape of an open umbrella. It is a small fountain, the second preserved from Turkish times in the city. There is no information who built the fountain. Some say it is the work of Kolyo Ficheto, others that it is a Turkish master.

Dospevska house

The Dospev House belonged to the family of the prominent Samokov painter Dimitar Hristov Zograf. His studio from 1848 is preserved in the yard. The house was restored in the 80s of the XX century and given to the Children's Art School "Zahari Zograf" for a creative workshop.
