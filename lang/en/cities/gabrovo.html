Bulgarian Cities - Gabrovo


СОФИЯ ВРЕМЕТО

Gabrovo residents are known for their love of humor, with their annual a humorous carnival of humor and satire, with its mechanical engineering, with its numerous monuments and bridges and long streets.


In Gabrovo you can find many sights such as


Home of humor and satire

If you like subtle jokes and unsurpassed humor, visit Gabrovo - the kingdom of laughter and fun. The people of Gabrovo still manage to set aside some levs to get the only one a worldwide home of humor and satire. Palace of Laughter will impress you with its 10 exhibitions halls, library, recording studio, souvenir stand and nightclub. Among his constants exhibitions you will see "The Roots of Gabrovo Humor", "Paradise", "Sin", "Planet Gabrovo", "The Mask - the face of the holiday "," Carnivals of the World ".

Interactive Museum of Industry

Traditional cultural symbols and modern technologies unite efforts in one unique in its essence and without an analogue throughout Bulgaria, a museum that reveals in an extremely fascinating way the history of the industrial apogee of Gabrovo, preserved behind its massive double doors with bronze fittings and handles lion heads. The interactive museum of industry attracts its visitors through the innovative one your approach and the possibilities of modern technology in time travel, translates them through different historical epochs, reveals before their eyes with their active participation exciting moments in the history of this emblematic Bulgarian city.

Aprilov National High School

The Aprilovo National High School was built in 1851, but became a monument to culture only in 1979. Since 1889 it has been called Aprilov High School after its founder. Vasil Aprilov. In front of the high school there is a monument to him from 1935. Vasil Aprilov introduced the mutual learning method, which makes Aprilov High School the first secular school in Bulgaria. Currently, Aprilov High School is a high school with a humanitarian and foreign language profile. The school is considered for one of the most elite in Gabrovo.

Clocktower

Elevated to 28 meters, the clock tower in the center of Gabrovo is one of its symbols. It was built in 1835. Its clockwork is handmade by a local master Ivan Sahatchiyata. And the bell of the majestic tower was made in Vienna.

National Museum of Education

Apart from humor and jokes, the town of Gabrovo is also known for being the only one of its kind in Bulgaria National Museum of Education. It is located in one of the wings of the first secular school in Bulgaria - Aprilov High School. Here you can trace the stages of Bulgarian education from the IX century to the present day. An interesting walk through the five halls of the museum awaits you.

Monument of Racho Kovacha

It is the only monument in our country built in the river. Known for their thrift, Gabrovo residents have chosen a special place in the Yantra River, so as not to waste living space and not to spend money for the presentation of flowers. According to legend, from the anvil of Racho Kovacha started the livelihood of the settlement and not long after Gabrovo became a craft center at that time.
